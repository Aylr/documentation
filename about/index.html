This blog will touch on many of the technical choices made at Health Catalyst, mainly around data science. If there’s a particular topic that interests, contact us! Some posts will be short, while others will be in-depth. The tone will be informal, with a focus on content and frequent posts (twice per-week) rather than polish. When we talk about doing things with data, we’ll post the code, so you can follow along. Please excuse any temporary site issues, as we're experimenting with what might work best.
